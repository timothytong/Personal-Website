About me.

Hello and welcome, this is Tim. I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.

I consider myself a technology enthusiast. Since I was young, I have always been fascinated by what technology is capable of doing - it makes our lives so much easier and more enjoyable! Hence, I decided that it shall be my ultimate goal to unwrap and learn all about this mystery.

As a result of being so passionate about technology, I am interested in working with both hardware and software. In my spare time, I take initiative to learn new languages, get my hands dirty on software and libraries that I never saw before, and write apps.

My aspiration is to be part of a team that engineers great products and makes impact on the world.
